Managed Cloud Services

Deployin offers a set of strategic services on the cloud such as, consultancy, implementation, security and system administration. We make use of public cloud services like Amazon Web Services or private clouds like OpenStack or Kubernetes. Besides, we have experience on DevOps techniques and tools such as Infrastructure as a Code, Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery. Furthermore, we know that putting your resources and data in the cloud requires an extensive integration of information security. For that reason, we take care of it, and we are able to implement controls to follow international compliances like HIPAA and PCI DSS.

Cloud Computing Engagement

Deployin offers integration and consultancy of tools which will allow to our customers manage their own infrastructure in an agile and self-healing fashion. Making lower operational loads of your information systems. This will allow to you and your team focus on the business. Our engagement services is based on the following principles:

  • Bootstrapping: Quick deployments.
  • Continuous Delivery: Agile and automatic deployment of system changes.
  • Monitoring: Automatic actions based on predefined alarms and conditions.
  • Security: Continuous security audits in each deployment.

Cloud Security

Under the premise of the DevOps culture we apply Continuous Security. This mean that security practices will be included from early stages of your infrastructure development and configuration. This will allow that security measures can be applied quick, easy and constantly adaptable to the infrastructure changes over the time. Deployin offers the following security services.

  • Continuous Security Scanning: security scanning on every deployment.
  • Dynamic Firewalls: it will adapt to infrastructure changes dynamically.
  • Disposable Servers: Immutable Infrastructure with finite life times.
  • PCI-DSS/HIPAA: accomplishment of security compliances and secure data management.

Disaster Recovery

Disaster Recovery is being able to quickly recover service operations in case of major disasters. Most frequent disaster issues in IT are. Hardware/Software failures, Network failures, Facilities physical damage, Power outages, Fire and Human errors.

Deployin helps you to being prepared for this events in an optimal, fast and low costs way. Operation recovering can be adapted to the customer magnitude and needs. It can be from recover just critical assets of your system or having a whole replica of your infrastructure that can be spawned in the cloud.
